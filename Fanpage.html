The Growlers - A Fan Page!

Bio:

The Growlers are a Alternative/Surf Rock band formed in Dana Point, California in 2006. Praised for their experimental and diverse sound their music blends elements of Surf Rock, Blues, Alternative and Folk creating a unique sound they've coined "Beach Goth". The Growlers limited touring to several circuits in their home state of California prior to achieving commercial success and international recognition after the release of thier breakthrough album, Hung at Heart. During their "golden years" from 2011-2016 the band received collaboration requests from several big artists, including Julian Casablancas of the Strokes and Dan Auerbach of the Black Keys. Throughout their career they have built an international following of fans, particularly in the United States, Australia, Spain, Mexico and Brazil.

In 2010 they relocated to Costa Mesa, CA to further refine their sound. In 2012 they founded the music festival, Beach Goth to support local artists. Currently the band has headlined the festival each year since it's inception, other notable guest performances included: Modest Mouse, Violent Femmes, Reel Big Fish, Gucci Mane, and Rocky Horror Picture Show.

To find out more check out their site here: thegrowlers.com
